Instructions

Concentration, also known as 'Match Match', 'Memory', 'Pelmanism', 'Shinkei-suijaku', 'Pexaso' or 'Pairs', is a card game in which all of the cards are laid facedown on a surface and two cards are flipped over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.

What is fun about this game?

Concentration aids in memory and enhances one's ability to focus. It increases observational powers and can in rare cases lead to super enlarged craniums and exotic mental powers.
